TODAY WE ARE TEACHING HOW TO PREPARE OGBONNO SOUP WITH VEGETABLE(UGU)

Ogbono soup is a popular Nigerian dish enjoyed across the continent with any swallow of choice.It is made from ogbono fruit seed , the collected dried under the sun and grinded for use .

Ogbonno soup ingredients:

  • ogbonno seed
  • Ogiri okpei
  • stock fish
  • ponmo
  • sharki
  • beef
  • fresh pepper
  • dry pepper(ghana pepper)
  • snails
  • crayfish
  • salt
  • seasoning cubes
  • onions
  • palm oil
  • Ugu(pumpkin leaves)

Preparation:

  1. Grinde the Ogbonno and Ogiri into smooth powder and set aside
  2. Soak the stock fish in hot water and shred, set aside
  3. Chop the ugu , set aside
  4. In a medium size pot ,boil the sharki,ponmo,snail,meat . add a slice of onion, three pinch of salt and two cubes of seasoning .Boil for 5-10 minutes
  5. Add water and cook for 30 minutes but remove the meat and snail so they don't over cook .Add stock fish,to the ponmo and sharki
  6. Heat palmoil in and empty pot for 30 seconds and add grinded pepper and start for 2min
  7. Turn down the heat from the pot and add the grinded ogbonno,ogiri and stir for 30 secs then add the meat stock,mix well.
  8. Add the cray fish,snail ,meat ,sharki,ponmo,2 cubes of seasoning and alittle water
  9. stir for 5-7min add the chopped Vegetable(ugu)
